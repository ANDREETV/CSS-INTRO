Focaccia with Caramelize Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste with this focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the focaccia
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
